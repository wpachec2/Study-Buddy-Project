Welcome to Study Buddy


This website is a tool to help you study. Users can input their flashcards, hold click over them in order to turn it over and see the answer.


Usage

Generating new question:

In order to generate a new question view the Questions page then press the "Enter New Question" to enter in a new question. Please enter in the appropirate question and answer forms, once finished please click the "Add Question" button to submit your question.

Favoriting:

This website allows users to favorite questions that they wish to view on their Favorites page. In order to favorite begin by assigning your UserId under the "Enter UserId to add Favorites:" section at the top of the questions page. Removing a question:

To remove a question please click on the "x" button at the top right of your flashcard.

Viewing Your Favorites

In order to view your favorited questions in the Favorites page enter in your UserId and click submit, this will show you all the flashcards which you have favorited.
